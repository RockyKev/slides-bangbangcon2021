TSHS Latin Club

The Seven Hills Latin Club strives to promote the classics at and around Seven Hills. Throughout the year, we have "Latin Days" when we educate the community in fun and exciting ways. In the winter and spring, we focus on the State OJCL Convention. Finally, in the late spring, we conclude the year with our celebration of National Latin Week.
